# Email Marketing Copy Portfolio

This repository contains sample email marketing campaigns written for fictional brands across ecommerce, SaaS, online education, wellness, home and garden, and B2B services.

The goal of this portfolio is to demonstrate practical email copywriting skills, including subject lines, preview text, campaign strategy, audience targeting, calls to action, and full email body copy.

## Campaigns Included

### Welcome Sequence

A five-email onboarding sequence for a fictional wellness and productivity app. This sequence introduces the brand, builds trust, explains the core value, and encourages the subscriber to take their first action.

### Product Launch Sequence

A five-email launch campaign for a fictional ecommerce software company. This sequence builds anticipation, announces the product, explains benefits, handles objections, and creates urgency.

### Abandoned Cart Sequence

A three-email ecommerce sequence for a fictional outdoor gear store. This campaign reminds shoppers about abandoned items, reinforces product value, and encourages checkout completion.

### Re-Engagement Sequence

A three-email campaign for a fictional online learning platform. This sequence is designed to reconnect with inactive subscribers and either bring them back or clean the email list.

### Newsletter Samples

Three newsletter samples for a fictional home and gardening brand. These samples show different newsletter formats, including founder updates, educational content, and promotional announcements.

### Sales Nurture Sequence

A five-email B2B nurture campaign for a fictional operations consulting company. This sequence moves a prospect from problem awareness to consultation booking.
