# Email 01: Welcome Email

## Campaign
Welcome Sequence

## Brand
CalmNest

## Goal
Welcome the subscriber, confirm they made a good decision, introduce the brand promise, and encourage them to take their first small action.

## Audience / Persona
New subscriber who feels overwhelmed and wants a simpler way to manage stress, focus, and daily routines.

## Subject Line Options
1. Welcome to CalmNest
2. You’re in — let’s make today feel lighter
3. Start with one calm step
4. Glad you’re here
5. Your calmer routine starts here

## Preview Text
Here’s the easiest way to begin building a calmer, more focused day.

## Email Body

Hi [First Name],

Welcome to CalmNest.

You’re here because you want your days to feel a little less scattered and a little more manageable.

That does not mean you need a perfect morning routine, a complicated productivity system, or another app that makes you feel behind before the day even starts.

CalmNest was built around a simpler idea:

Small moments of calm can change the shape of your whole day.

Inside CalmNest, you can create simple routines that help you:

- Start your day with more focus
- Pause when stress builds up
- Organize your priorities without overthinking them
- End the day feeling more grounded

The best place to start is with your first Calm Routine. It only takes a few minutes, and you can adjust it anytime.

[CTA Button: Start Your First Calm Routine]

Thanks for being here. We’ll send a few short emails over the next few days to help you get the most out of CalmNest.

You do not have to change your whole life today.

Just start with one calm step.

Best,  
The CalmNest Team

## CTA
Start Your First Calm Routine

## Strategy Notes
This email uses a warm and reassuring tone. The goal is not to sell immediately. The goal is to reduce hesitation, make the subscriber feel understood, and move them toward one simple activation step.
