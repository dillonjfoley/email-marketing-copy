# Email 04: Social Proof Email

## Campaign
Welcome Sequence

## Brand
CalmNest

## Goal
Build trust by showing how different types of users benefit from CalmNest.

## Audience / Persona
Subscriber who is curious but may need reassurance that CalmNest can work for someone with a busy, imperfect schedule.

## Subject Line Options
1. How people are using CalmNest
2. Real routines. Real life. Less stress.
3. You do not need a perfect schedule
4. See how CalmNest fits into busy days
5. Small routines, real results

## Preview Text
Here are a few ways CalmNest users create calmer days without overhauling their lives.

## Email Body

Hi [First Name],

“I use CalmNest before I open my email. It helps me decide what actually matters before the day gets loud.”

## Strategy Notes
This email uses testimonial-style examples to make the product feel credible and relatable. The proof is practical rather than flashy, which fits the calm and supportive brand voice.
